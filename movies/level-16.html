Level 16

Level 16 is a 2018 science fiction horror by filmmaker Danishka Esterhazy. It follows a group of girls who live at a "school" which educates them about how to be perfect young women for families that they are told will eventually adopt them. Two girls work together to uncover the truth about their captivity.

The plot and overall premise of the film shows many similarities to the 2005 film The Island and Kazuo Ishiguro 's 2005 novel Never Let Me Go.
